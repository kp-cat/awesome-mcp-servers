## Overview

Block (formerly Square) provides an MCP server enabling AI agents to interact with payment processing, point-of-sale systems, and financial services. As a co-founder of the Agentic AI Foundation (AAIF) under the Linux Foundation alongside Anthropic and OpenAI, Block is committed to advancing AI agent capabilities in financial services.

## Features

### Payment Processing
- **Transaction Management**: Process payments, refunds, and voids
- **Payment Methods**: Credit cards, digital wallets, cash
- **Multi-Currency**: Support for global payments
- **Recurring Billing**: Subscription and recurring payment management

### Point of Sale
- **POS Operations**: Sales, refunds, and exchanges
- **Inventory Management**: Track stock and products
- **Employee Management**: Staff permissions and shifts
- **Customer Profiles**: Customer data and purchase history

### Business Operations
- **Analytics**: Sales reports and business insights
- **Customer Engagement**: Loyalty programs and marketing
- **Team Management**: Employee scheduling and payroll
- **Multi-Location**: Manage multiple business locations
