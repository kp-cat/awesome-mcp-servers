## Overview

ChatterBox is a specialized MCP server that enables AI agents to automatically join online meetings, capture conversations, and provide transcripts and recordings for further processing and analysis.

## Features

- **Automated Meeting Attendance**: Send AI bots to Zoom and Google Meet meetings
- **Automatic Transcription**: Real-time and post-meeting transcription
- **Meeting Recordings**: Capture full meeting recordings
- **Multi-Platform Support**: Works with Zoom and Google Meet
- **AI-Powered Analysis**: Enable AI agents to process meeting content
- **Workflow Integration**: Connect meetings to automated workflows

## Capabilities

- Join meetings automatically via calendar integration
- Real-time speech-to-text transcription
- Post-meeting recording access
- Speaker identification and diarization
- Action item extraction
- Meeting summary generation
- Integration with note-taking systems

## Use Cases

- Automated meeting note-taking
- Sales call analysis and CRM updates
- Customer support call logging
- Training and onboarding documentation
- Compliance and record-keeping
- Meeting accessibility for remote team members
- Post-meeting action item tracking

## Supported Platforms

- Zoom Meetings
- Google Meet

## Workflow Integration

Enable AI agents to:
- Summarize key meeting points
- Extract action items and deadlines
- Update CRM systems with meeting notes
- Generate follow-up emails
