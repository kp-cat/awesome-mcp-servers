## Overview

PostcardBot is a unique MCP server that bridges the digital and physical worlds by enabling AI agents to send real physical postcards worldwide through natural language commands.

## Features

- **Worldwide Delivery**: Send physical postcards to recipients globally
- **Bulk Sending**: Support for up to 500 recipients per campaign
- **Volume Pricing**: Starting from $0.72 per card for bulk orders
- **AI-Powered Personalization**: Use AI agents to customize postcard content
- **Automated Campaigns**: Schedule and automate physical mail marketing

## Use Cases

- Physical marketing campaigns automated by AI
- Customer appreciation and retention programs
- Event reminders and invitations
- Personal correspondence at scale
- Direct mail campaigns with AI personalization
- Holiday greetings and seasonal campaigns

## How It Works

1. AI agent receives request to send postcards
2. Content and recipient details provided via MCP
3. PostcardBot processes and prints postcards
4. Physical postcards mailed to recipients
5. Tracking and delivery confirmation available

## Pricing

Volume-based pricing:
- Starting at $0.72 per card for bulk orders
- Pricing scales with volume
- Worldwide shipping included

## Integration

Works with MCP-compatible AI assistants for automated physical mail workflows.
