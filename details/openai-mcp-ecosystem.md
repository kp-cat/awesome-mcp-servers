## Overview

OpenAI is a co-founder of the Agentic AI Foundation (AAIF), a directed fund under the Linux Foundation, alongside Anthropic and Block. This foundation governs the Model Context Protocol, enabling ChatGPT and GPT-based applications to connect to MCP servers for enhanced AI agent capabilities.
